Types of Spacecrafts

Exploring the Wonders of Spacecrafts

From Exploration to Innovation

Orbital Spacecraft

Designed to orbit planets or celestial bodies for research and observation.

Interplanetary Spacecraft

Travel beyond Earth's orbit to explore other planets and deep space.

Rovers

Mobile robotic spacecraft used to explore the surface of celestial bodies.

Space Probes

Unmanned spacecraft designed to collect data from space environments.

Space Stations

Human-made structures designed for long-term space habitation and research.
